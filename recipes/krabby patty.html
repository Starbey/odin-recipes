Krabby Patty

Description

The Krusty Krab's flagship sandwich! Loved by every fish, crustacean, seasponge, and cephalopod in Bikini Bottom!

NOTE: Make sure to peridodically check under the counter for a certain green phytoplankton.

Ingredients

Burger Patty

  • 1 1/3 lb. ground beef
  • 1 tsp. black pepper
  • 1 tsp. sea salt
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • 1 sesame bun

Toppings

  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • 2 sliced pickles
  • 1 sliced red onion
  • 1 sliced tomato
  • 1 lettuce leaf
