Scooby Snacks

Danger ahead? Mysteries to be solved? Zoinks! ould you do it for a Scooby Snack? Fuel your investigative mind with Scooby Snacks!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Oatmeal
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Walnut extract

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven. Spray a baking sheet.
  2. Add all the ingredients into a bowl.
  3. Flatten the dough and cut into circular biscuits.
  4. Bake for 8 minutes.
  5. Zoinks Scoob! Enjoy!
