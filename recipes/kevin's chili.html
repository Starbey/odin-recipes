Kevin's Famous Chili

You'll want a taste of Kevin's World Famous Chili. It was passed down for generations.

We promise you'll fall head over heels for this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Pinto beans
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef stock
  • Tomatoes
  • Chili peppers
  • Yellow onions
  • Garlic
  • Ground cumin
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Sea salt
  • Scallions

Procedure

  1. Blend spices in a bowl.
  2. Finely dice your vegetables.
  3. Preheat oil and cook the vegetables in a stock pot.
  4. Add onions, peppers, and beans to your stock pot. Don't overcook the onions.
  5. Add chopped garlic, salt, and cayenne pepper.
  6. Stir for 2-5 minutes.
  7. Add a a few tablespoons of tomato paste and tomatoes.
  8. Cook for 5 minutes
  9. Add ground beef, your spice mix, and beef stock. Simmer over medium heat. Cook until your stew is reduced to half and thickened.
  10. Garnish your chili with toppings of your choosing.
  11. Enjoy! Don't trip on your way out.
